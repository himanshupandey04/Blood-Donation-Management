How It Works

Simple Process, Big Impact

1. Registration

Sign up and fill out a quick health history questionnaire to ensure eligibility.

2. Screening

A mini-physical checkup to measure pulse, blood pressure, and hemoglobin levels.

3. Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. Relax while you save lives.

4. Refreshment

Enjoy a snack and drink to replenish your energy before you continue your day.

Why Donate?

The Gift of Life

Blood cannot be manufactured. It can only come from generous donors. Your contribution is essential for surgeries, cancer treatments, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries.

  • One donation can help save up to 3 patients.
  • Free health screening with every donation.
  • It's a quick, safe, and simple process.
